Know the Canadian Immigration Prediction for the year 2021

Home Know the Canadian Immigration Prediction for the year 2021
28Aug
By Admin 35 Comments July 21, 2020

2020 was a pretty hard year for immigrants. After COVID-19 hit the world, everything including immigration invites came to the halt. It was also a noteworthy year in migration as it made Canada realise how important the immigrants are for their economy and that is why in 2021, the Canadian government has decided to increase the number of invites.

This not us speaking but is validated by the public authority of Canada through a notice. Thereby, the authority has promised to call in more migrants. So if you are looking to immigrate to Canada in 2021, then you can happily look forward to get selected this year!

In 2021, one of the major improvements Canada is planning is to get rid of unwieldy and obsolete paper-based Management System and get it upgraded with to an advanced computerized system of inviting candidates. This new system, as per the public authority, will improve the experience of the applicants, as well enhances the operational productivity and program trustworthiness. Candidates can also expect a quicker processing, as no time will be wasted in recurrent documentation checking and a more streamlined cycle to submit reports will be used pretty soon! With the Canadian government promising such exciting changes, isn’t this the right time to make Canada your permanent spot to settle down? It definitely is!

Speaking of non-PR migrants i.e., students on study visa, you all have pretty good chances too! As per the Canadian authority, prior to the pandemic, there were very limited international students who have joined up with online college courses. But COVID-19 has opened a new domain in the field of studying internationally. Since COVID, that number of online students has risen drastically. This means a more inclusive system of studying internationally. The Canadian government has been striving to guarantee that students learn in a ways better approach in Canada. Many students have missed a term of school, or a semester of college, but still the country is actively calling out to all those students who want to study in Canada. You can apply for Post-Graduate Work Permit (PGWP) or other short-term study visas by getting in touch with us today. This year is very crucial for study, as you have already missed on a lot. Do not wait for the colleges or schools to re-open in your area and enrol yourself in the top universities across Canada! Canada is known for its quality of education and it couldn’t be a better year to materialise your dream of having a great degree in a fab course!

Another good news for the skilled workers category people is that Canada’s Multi-year immigration plans have elevated its targets for 2021. This is because in 2020, a couple of months, no draw were conducted by Canada. So in order to meet its promised deadline of inviting skilled candidates, this year more invites have been decided for allotments. In the month of October, Canada's minister of immigration declared another multi-year levels plan that sees Canada inviting 401,000 new applicants in 2021, the best number of invitations to Canada ever! It was held that entry in Canada was restricted in 2020 because of the COVID-19 pandemic and that is why number of applicants expected to be called by the Canadian authority is much higher in 2021. It has also been said that this increased number of applicants would be called through Canada’s most popular program Express Entry via issuing ITAs to the selected skilled articles.

All in all, the previous year put may limitations and difficulties for immigration seekers, yet this year (2021) is all set for a record-breaking invites! If you see the last few draws of this year, you must have noticed higher quantities of ITAs being issued to the applicants from the Express entry pool. This trend is expected to go like this only for the up-coming draws of 2021. In 2021, we envision that this pattern can also get more sharped and increasingly more number of applicants will be called by the government of Canada.

All things considered, 2021 seems to be a great year for Canada immigration. So if you want to get started with your immigration application this year, then apply now without wasting any time! Call us today to talk to our immigration experts!
