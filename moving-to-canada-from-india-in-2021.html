Moving to Canada from India in 2021

Home Moving to Canada from India in 2021
28Aug
By Admin 35 Comments July 21, 2020

Immigration to Canada from India dates back to when the first Punjabi Sikh family migrated to Canada. They settled in the Canadian environment and communities so effortlessly that seeing them happily enjoying their life, many Indian families followed in their footsteps. The reasons for the migrants might be different but the sole purpose remains the same and that is, seeing themselves settled in the friendliest country.

Migrating to the Maple land is a great opportunity for people from Asian countries and specifically India to seek better jobs, life, individual growth, and government benefits. Not only the migrants are getting benefits here but the Canadian economy is also getting a boost. The Indo-Canadians are helping combat the lack of skills of jobs in Canada as well as contributing to government taxes, buying houses, using public transport, purchasing goods, and over-all stimulating the Canadian government.

Some years back, people used to flock to Toronto and Vancouver as they are the most commercialised provinces of Canada. Still, these two provinces are hugely popular specifically for those who are skilled and looking for business opportunities but slowly and steadily, there is a shift towards other Canadian provinces as well.

The interesting thing to note here is that a very large and dynamic group of Indian immigrants are living in the Toronto area, which even has a neighbourhood called “Little India”.

There is no doubt stating that Canada has become one of the most favourite destinations in the world for Indians. Canada has always welcomed immigrants with wide-open arms who are looking for better opportunities for employment, individual growth and of course the standard of living.

Canada is a melting pot for Indian migrants and there are various reasons to support why immigrants consider immigrating to Canada from India.

Reasons for Immigrating to Canada from India
  1. The only country to invite more than 250,000 new permanent residents every year. With the pandemic, the country might have invited the low numbers this year but the zeal is still intact and the Canadian government is trying every possible effort to invite as many immigrants as possible (as per the immigration new numbers set for invitation).
  2. Canada has invested in programs to help the newcomers settle into Canada and adapt to their new surroundings. These programs will help connect newcomers to a community of people to help build lasting relationships.
  3. The OECD Better Life Index rated Canada 9 on a 1-10 scale for safety. This means that the country knows how to take care of its population. Canada is a great place to raise a family in safe and secure communities.
  4. Canada has a world-class education system and thus invests hugely in this sector. Always considered for a place in the top five of the world, Canada ranks in top-10 when it comes to education. The country typically has better trained, well-paid teachers who also receive good job security and unionization.
  5. Canada is a country that has a strong economy with plenty of opportunities for skilled workers. Just barely behind the USA, Canada is expected to be the second strongest economy mainly because of the growing businesses in the oil market and the commodity market for producers.
  6. For the seventh consecutive year, Canada is the most stable banking system in the world. The Canadian banks are more reluctant to lend to home buyers with low to no credit, resulting in fewer mortgage defaults.
  7. Canada has high-tech infrastructure facilities with great medical facilities and high-rise buildings
  8. Canada has an amazing standard of living. The healthcare facilities are great and if you become a permanent resident, you are entitled to free medical services in the government hospitals. When it comes to women empowerment, Canada stands tall here as well. Women participate in government functioning and also have guaranteed paid maternity leave. Life expectancy for Canadians sits at 80.9 years with fewer instances of medical history. In other words, Canada has been soaring above other countries with the quality of life.
Indians are moving to Canada at a faster rate than any other country!!

Since 2016 there was a 105% increase of Indians admitted as Permanent Residents of Canada. Unlike the US, Canada has streamlined its work permits for tech workers to help provide them with a faster path to Canadian permanent residency.

Also since 2016, there was a 127% increase in Indian International Students in Canada. The country makes it much easier to transform international students into permanent residents. While Canada’s Indian international student rate increases, the US’s rate has decreased by more than 10%.

How Can I Immigrate to Canada from India?

If you are currently living in India and want to move to Canada, there are many various options for immigration programs available for you. The Canadian Immigration process is different for different programs and a single mistake can ruin your chances of becoming a permanent resident of Canada. The process can be tricky and confusing but hiring a qualified Canadian immigration consultancy can help you in applying for Canadian Permanent Residency. These experts are pro in their expertise and thus, your chances of becoming a permanent resident become threefold.
